Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about my services and work process.

The process starts with a consultation to understand your needs, followed by sending a proposal and offer. After an agreement, we will enter the project execution phase with regular communication until the project is completed and delivered.

The completion time varies greatly depending on the project's complexity. A logo design project usually takes 1-2 weeks, while a photo retouching project can be completed in a few days. A more accurate time estimate will be provided in the proposal.

For new projects, I usually require a 50% down payment before work begins. The remaining payment is made after the project is completed and before the final files are delivered. I accept payments via bank transfer.
